About Ben

Melbourne DJ for Weddings & Private Events.

Based in Melbourne, Ben brings over a decade of DJ experience to weddings, birthdays, private functions, bars, and large-scale events. Known for his ability to read the room and adapt in real time, he tailors every set to the crowd and atmosphere to create a seamless, memorable night from start to finish.

Ben has performed at a wide range of events, from intimate celebrations to major corporate functions, including work with organisations such as the AFL and Formula 1. Professional, reliable, and easy to work with, he focuses on delivering an experience that feels effortless for clients and unforgettable for guests.
